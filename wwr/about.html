About Us

At Rogue River Rafting Co, we believe the best memories are made with wet clothes, wild stories, and wide-open skies. We're a passionate crew of guides, explorers, and river lovers based in the heart of Utah, where the canyons run deep and the rapids run wild. Whether you're a first-time paddler or a seasoned adventurer, our trips are designed to get your heart racing and your soul refreshed. With safety as our anchor and fun as our compass, we're here to help you paddle through unforgettable experiences—one rapid at a time.

Long before highways crisscrossed the desert, Utah’s rivers carved the state’s lifelines through canyons and valleys. For centuries, the Colorado, Green, and San Juan Rivers served as essential routes for Indigenous peoples, traders, and explorers. These waterways shaped the landscape and the communities that formed around them. By the late 1800s, rivers like the Green became popular with daring geologists and adventurers seeking to map the uncharted West. Today, their legacy flows on—not just as vital natural resources, but as thrilling destinations for adventure and discovery.

History

Rogue River Rafting Co began in 1983 with one inflatable raft, a beat-up truck, and a dream to turn river thrills into unforgettable memories. Founded by two childhood friends who spent summers paddling the choppy rapids of southern Utah, the company started as a weekend passion and quickly grew into a full-fledged operation. Known for their deep local knowledge and contagious enthusiasm, Rogue River Rafting Co has guided thousands of adventurers through swirling eddies and sun-drenched canyons—always with a laugh, a splash, and a story to take home.
