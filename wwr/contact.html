Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What should I bring for a rafting trip?

A: Water shoes or sandals with straps, sunscreen, a change of clothes, snacks or a meal and a towel. We'll provide helmets, water, life jackets, rafts and paddles.

Q: Do I need experience to go rafting?

A: Nope! We have trips for all experience levels. Our guides provide full safety briefings and instructions.

Q: Is there an age or weight limit?

A: Yes. Minimum age is 7 years old. Participants must weigh between 50–275 lbs for safety reasons.

Q: What happens if the weather is bad?

A: Safety is our top priority. We may delay or reschedule trips in severe weather. You'll receive a full refund or option to rebook.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

At Rogue River Rafting Co., your safety and satisfaction are our top priorities. We understand that plans can change, so we’ve created a fair and flexible cancellation policy to accommodate unexpected situations.

Cancellation Timeframes & Refunds

- More than 4 days before your trip:

✅ Full refund or option to reschedule at no charge.

5 to 7 days before your trip:

🔁 Free rescheduling OR
💰 50% refund if cancelling.

Less than 96 hours before your trip or no-shows:

❌ No refund. Trips are non-refundable due to staffing, scheduling and equipment commitments.
💡 We recommend travel insurance for added flexibility.
